## V005 Design

V005 isolates one terrain-assisted attack in one stable area.

Core design:

- Stable area: rainy exterior corridor / columns / railing / wet stone floor.
- Active references remain the three upload-safe JPG refs:
  - `@FENSHOU_UPLOADSAFE` for Fenshou identity only.
  - `@SHUANGJI_UPLOADSAFE` for Shuangji identity only.
  - `@TEMPLE_SCENE_UPLOADSAFE` for rainy temple exterior corridor world only.
- Main action: Fenshou plants one boot on a low column base, low wall edge, or railing support.
- The boot contact is short, physical, and visible.
- Water squeezes from the wet surface.
- The foot plant changes Fenshou's attack angle.
- Fenshou drives a diagonal downward elbow / forearm / hammerfist into Shuangji's crossed guard.
- Shuangji reacts with guard compression, shoulder dip, body displacement, rear-foot skid, and water splash.
- The fight continues immediately after the terrain-assisted impact.

Boundaries:

- One main terrain-assisted attack only.
- One stable exterior corridor area only.
- No structural breakage.
- No roof.
- No full wall-run.
- No floating.
- No wire-fu.
- No full building destruction.
- No smoke-hole suspense yet.

- Full architectural impact, broken entry, smoke, dust, and hole-in-wall suspense are stronger candidates for SHOT-04.
- SHOT-04 should split controlled architectural impact into multiple 5-second shots rather than packing all escalation ideas into one generation.

ChatGPT should audit the V005 manual prompt for:

- First clear contact timing within `0.15s-0.30s`.
- Terrain foot plant clarity.
- Whether the boot visibly contacts a low column base, low wall edge, or railing support.
- Whether the body angle changes because of that terrain contact.
- No floating, no full wall-run, no roof transition.
- Main terrain-assisted impact clarity.
- Contact beat count of at least 6 clear beats.
