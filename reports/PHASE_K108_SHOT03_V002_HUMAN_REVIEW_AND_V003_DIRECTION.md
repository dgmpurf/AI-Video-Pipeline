## Diagnosis

The V002 package tried to solve V001 continuity by including too many route elements in one generation:

exterior veranda -> wet stairs -> eaves / roof edge -> local breakage.

This overloaded the model. The roof/eaves endpoint caused the model to jump too far too quickly, and the fast close motion damaged facial stability. V002 should therefore be treated as a failure reference, not as a final or usable direction reference.
