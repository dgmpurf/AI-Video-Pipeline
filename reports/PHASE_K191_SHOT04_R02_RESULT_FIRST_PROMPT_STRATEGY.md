## 6. Diagnosis

K185/K186 passed package review and clause-level Source compliance, but visual success did not follow.

The failed prompt was too constraint-first and audit-oriented. It emphasized:

- compressing guard toward railing/lattice
- localized flex
- reduced crack priority
- keeping railing standing
- avoiding static pushing

Those are valid constraints, but they were not strong enough as a primary visual result. The phrase `compresses guard toward railing/lattice` was too soft. The model interpreted the action as pushing/holding, creating sustained pressure or wrestling-like motion instead of one clear architecture-as-combat impact.

Future prompt logic must start with a high-priority visual result sentence, then constrain and audit that result afterward.

## 7. Result-First Prompt Principle

New prompt order:

1. Result sentence
2. Physical hit path
3. Wall/contact consequence
4. Rebound/counter
5. Timing schedule
6. Negative constraints

The first sentence should tell the model what image/action must be seen before explaining what must not happen.

Core result-first sentence:

```text
Fenshou lands one explosive shoulder-check into Shuangji's crossed guard and slams her back-first into a wooden wall panel.
```

## 8. SHOT-04 R02 Concept

Name:

`wooden wall-panel slam / back-first rebound`

Premise:

Fenshou's explosive shoulder-check hits Shuangji's crossed guard and drives her back-first into a wooden wall panel. The panel dents/flexes locally with rainwater and tiny splinters, then Shuangji rebounds and counters.

The goal is not broad destruction. The goal is one clear physical combat result, one local architecture consequence, and one immediate rebound.

## 9. Composition Strategy

Reuse the same general rainy corridor / temple side corridor world, but shift the architecture target:

- from railing/lattice line
- to wooden wall panel or wooden lattice wall panel

Composition requirements:

- Fenshou left-center, driving rightward.
- Shuangji right-center, near a wooden wall panel.
- Wooden wall panel on the right midground, readable as the impact target.
- Wet stone foot skid zone remains visible.
- Both faces remain readable enough for identity.
- No extreme close-up.
- No flat side-scroller view.
- No roof route, wall run, or broad location change.

## 10. Draft Prompt Blueprint

This is a blueprint only, not a prompt txt file.

### Main Result Sentence

```text
Fenshou lands one explosive shoulder-check into Shuangji's crossed guard and slams her back-first into a wooden wall panel.
```

### Controlled Impact

```text
Her shoulder and upper back hit the panel hard. The panel makes a shallow inward dent / local flex at the exact contact point, with a burst of rainwater and two tiny wood splinters.
```

### Wall Safety

```text
The wooden wall panel remains standing. No wall collapse. No body embedded inside the wall. No body-wall fusion. No gore.
```

### Rebound

```text
Shuangji immediately rebounds off the panel and counters into Fenshou's guard.
```

### Integrated Blueprint Paragraph

```text
Create a 5-second cinematic semi-realistic 3D wuxia combat video in the rainy ancient temple side corridor. Fenshou is the black-red male fighter on the left-center side. Shuangji is the blue-white / silver female fighter on the right-center side near a wooden wall panel. Fenshou lands one explosive shoulder-check into Shuangji's crossed guard and slams her back-first into the wooden wall panel. Her shoulder and upper back hit the panel hard. The panel makes a shallow inward dent / local flex only at the exact contact point, with a burst of rainwater and two tiny wood splinters. The wooden wall panel remains standing. Shuangji immediately rebounds off the panel and counters into Fenshou's guard. Both fighters stay engaged through the final frame.
```
