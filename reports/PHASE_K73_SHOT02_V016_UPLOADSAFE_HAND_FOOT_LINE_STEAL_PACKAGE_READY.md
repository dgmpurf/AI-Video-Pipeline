## V016 Purpose

V016 is the second clip in the long fast-combat plan. It is a hand-foot escalation and line-stealing correction package.

The clip should:

- begin with close hand exchange
- show that hand exchange alone cannot break through
- escalate into lower-body footwork
- use front-foot cut-in and diagonal outside-angle step
- introduce low kick / ankle sweep threat
- show shin check or knee line seal
- force off-line balance recovery
- immediately re-enter pressure
- continue fighting through the final second

## V016 Action Summary

The planned 5-second structure:

1. 0.00s-0.70s: close hand exchange begins; Fenshou presses with compact forearm pressure; Shuangji parries and checks the line; do not stay in repeated hand-blocking rhythm.
2. 0.70s-1.50s: Fenshou cuts his front foot inside to steal the centerline, driving from rear foot and hip rotation; Shuangji steps diagonally outside.
3. 1.50s-2.40s: Fenshou threatens low kick or ankle sweep toward Shuangji front leg; Shuangji lifts the knee slightly or turns the shin outward to seal the line while upper body continues forearm parry.
4. 2.40s-3.40s: contact forces one fighter slightly off-line; shoe skids on wet stone; small water splash moves sideways; robe hem and sleeves snap from the sudden weight shift; extremely short hit-stop only at key contact, then immediate rebound.
5. 3.40s-5.00s: off-line fighter recovers balance with low stance or side step, then immediately re-enters with palm/forearm pressure and footwork; the fight continues through the final second.

## V016 Force And Impact Summary

Power must come from body mechanics and footwork, not from large visual effects.

Required impact language:

- true-speed fast combat
- not soft fast motion
- not slow-motion-only impact
- grounded stance

- hip/waist rotation
- shoulder-driven compact strike
- front-foot cut-in
- diagonal outside-angle step

- shin check / lower-leg block
- knee line seal
- ankle / foot sweep threat without acrobatics
- short contact and immediate rebound
- 0.1s-0.2s hit-stop only at key contact
- visible balance recovery
- immediate re-entry
- wet stone shoe skid
- small water splash
- robe hem flick
- sleeve snap
- hair whip
- continuous pressure

## V016 Micro-FX Summary

Micro-FX are allowed only as small contact and environment feedback:

- tiny red-blue contact sparks at forearm or shin-check collision
- short silver-blue streak at Shuangji block edge
- dark-red ember trace on Fenshou pressure attack
- cold glowing rain splash from low footwork
- brief contact flicker under 0.2s

Micro-FX must not become the main source of impact. The impact must read from stance, weight shift, contact, legwork, and rebound.

Forbidden effects:

- giant shockwave
- new giant shockwave
- second explosion
- circular water shield
- energy shield dome
- full-body aura burst
- large spell effect
- big magic beam

## Camera And Rhythm Summary

Use medium close combat framing. When legwork begins, the camera may slightly widen or dip so both characters remain visible from upper body to knees.

The rainy temple frontal axis must remain readable. The camera may use subtle combat-following micro-adjustments, but should avoid spinning, beauty-portrait drift, static poster drift, or fast cuts that destroy body mechanics.
